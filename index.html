Types of Kimchi

  • Baechu-kimchi: (Most known type) Uses whole napa cabbage leaves and ferments them in a spicy mixture
  • Kkakdugi: A crunchy kimchi made from cubed Korean radishes.
  • Pa-kimchi: Slightly bitter kimchi made from green onions.
  • Dongchimi: Watery and white kimchi made from radishes. It has a clean and refreshing taste.
  • Oi-sobagi: Refreshing kimchi made from cucumbers. It is perfect for the summer!
